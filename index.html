Rules

1. **Teamwork is Key**: Coordinate with your team, communicate effectively, and work together to achieve victory.

2. **Respect Your Teammates**: Treat your teammates with respect and avoid toxic behavior or harassment.

3. **Know Your Role**: Understand the role of your hero and fulfill your duties accordingly. Whether you're a tank, marksman, mage, support, or assassin, play to your strengths.

4. **Map Awareness**: Keep an eye on the mini-map to track enemy movements, objectives, and potential ganks.

5. **Farm Efficiently**: Prioritize farming to level up and gain gold, but also be ready to assist teammates when needed.

6. **Objective Control**: Secure objectives such as turrets, jungle monsters, and the Lord or Turtle to gain an advantage for your team.

7. **Itemization**: Build items that complement your hero's strengths and counter the enemy team's composition.

8. **Stay Alive**: Avoid unnecessary deaths as each death gives the enemy team an advantage in gold and experience.

9. **Adaptability**: Be flexible and willing to adjust your strategy based on the flow of the game and your team's needs.

10. **Never Surrender**: Keep fighting until the end. Comebacks are always possible, and surrendering prematurely can cost you the game.
